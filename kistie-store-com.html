About Kistie Store

Fashionably Sorted

Kistie Store is more than just a fashion boutique - we're a lifestyle destination dedicated to helping you express your unique style. With carefully curated collections from emerging designers and established brands, we bring you the best in contemporary fashion.

Our mission is simple: to make premium fashion accessible to everyone. We believe that great style shouldn't break the bank, and that everyone deserves to feel confident and beautiful in what they wear.

Based in Kampala, Uganda, we serve fashion enthusiasts across the region with the same passion and dedication that started it all. Join our growing community of style-savvy customers.
